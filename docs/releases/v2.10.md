## Review before implementation

Three independent reviewer agents examined the v2.9.1 build before changes:

| Reviewer lane | Agent | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Difficulty math | Kuhn | nine-level curve, discontinuities, damage/flasks/poise |
| Adversarial combat | McClintock | record integrity, live mutation, phase/combo timing |
| Player panel | Wegener | onboarding, fairness, perceived difficulty, mobile readability |

Every accepted claim was reproduced on both local and production v2.9.1:

- A run could begin at +5, mutate the live grace value to −3, receive −3 damage, iframe,
  perfect-window, and stagger behavior, yet still write a +5 record.
- Phase two used three swipes; phase three regressed to two.
- At +5, the phase-two follow-up hit arrived in about 204 ms while its renderer began roughly
  38% through a separately reconstructed 331 ms telegraph. Phase three fell to about 184 ms.
- No-stagger appeared abruptly at +3 even though the gold poise bar still promised a break.
- The second flask disappeared at +4, stacking another discrete cliff before +5.
- A selected +5 record of 2:00 rendered as a global 0:40 best.
- `FORSAKEN +5` overlapped its last title-menu pip by about 10 px.

The review also established what should remain:

- the strongest ordinary +5 direct hit was 36 damage against 110 HP, not a one-shot;
- post-hit invulnerability prevented one burst from landing repeatedly;
- phase transitions cleared stale projectiles, rings, and meteors;
- projectile and expanding-ring travel remained readable because it did not inherit the boss
  animation-speed multiplier;
- the existing sparse MiniMax score, action SFX mix, floor cache, particle budget, and arena
  rendering did not require replacement.

## Shipped curve

Speed and incoming damage preserve the existing linear ramps. Flask and poise thresholds were
redistributed so +3/+4 are demanding but still support the core stagger loop; no-stagger belongs
only to the named +5 capstone.

| Trial | Boss speed | Damage taken | Roll iframe multiplier | Flasks | Boss poise | Stagger opening |
|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| −3 | 0.78× | 0.55× | 1.55× | 4 | 120 | 1.70 s |
| −2 | 0.85× | 0.70× | 1.37× | 4 | 120 | 1.70 s |
| −1 | 0.93× | 0.85× | 1.18× | 3 | 120 | 1.70 s |
| 0 | 1.00× | 1.00× | 1.00× | 3 | 120 | 1.70 s |
| +1 | 1.06× | 1.08× | 1.00× | 3 | 120 | 1.70 s |
| +2 | 1.12× | 1.16× | 1.00× | 2 | 120 | 1.70 s |
| +3 | 1.18× | 1.24× | 1.00× | 2 | 162 | 1.45 s |
| +4 | 1.24× | 1.32× | 1.00× | 2 | 204 | 1.25 s |
| +5 | 1.30× | 1.40× | 1.00× | 1 | 204 | IRONBOUND |

Implementation invariants:

- `difficultyForGrace()` is the only authoring point for these values.
- `resetFight()` copies one modifier object into `trialMods`.
- `Game.mods` returns that immutable snapshot outside the title.
- Canvas and semantic trial controls reject changes during intro/fight.
- The victory key remains `graceAtStart`, so the HUD, live combat, and saved record describe
  one setting.
- The title uses `bests[selectedGrace]`; the legacy top-level PB is a grace-0 migration fallback.

## Combat and UI changes

- Phase two and phase three both begin swipe strings with three attacks.
- Touch fresh windups have a 300 ms minimum; swipe follow-ups have a 240 ms minimum.
- `Boss.currentWindup` is the single duration read by both hit logic and telegraph rendering.
- Projectile and ring travel speed is unchanged.
- +5 renders `IRONBOUND` beside the phase pips and replaces the fillable poise sliver with a
  segmented locked rail.
- Grace pips moved left inside the existing menu plate. The measured +5 text clearance is 7.935 px
  against a six-pixel minimum.

## Automated acceptance

The same `qa/verify.cjs` contract ran in three places:

| Environment | Revision | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Local isolated server | release candidate | PASS, zero errors |
| GitHub Actions `playtest` | rebased PR head `9e09a4b` | PASS in 1m23s |
| Public production rerun | merged `8467979` | PASS, zero errors |

The release-specific assertions cover:

- all nine grace settings and strictly increasing speed/damage;
- exact flask and poise tables;
- +5-only no-stagger;
- preview-to-active modifier equality;
- mid-fight semantic and direct-mutation resistance;
- 28 damage from a base-20 +5 hit;
- +3 breakable poise with a 1.45 s opening;
- +5 reset-without-stagger behavior;
- selected-trial record lookup;
- phase-two and phase-three three-swipe starts;
- exact visual/combat timing at 330.529/240 ms in phase two and 300/240 ms in phase three;
- measured `FORSAKEN +5` clearance and visible `IRONBOUND` copy;
- existing desktop, mobile, real-touch, audio, terminal, palette, performance, and
  death/retry regressions.

The accepted production rerun measured first-tap audio initialization at 14 ms. An earlier public
run passed every gameplay and difficulty assertion but measured this timing-sensitive existing
audio guard at 20.4 ms against its strict `<20 ms` threshold. The full fresh rerun—not a reduced
probe—then passed at 14 ms. Both results are recorded here rather than hiding the transient miss.

## Known limits

- Headless Chromium can verify that vibration is requested, but not the physical feel of haptics.
- Real iOS Safari URL-bar/dynamic-viewport behavior still needs a device check.
- Automated audio checks validate routing, cue admission, timing, voice pressure, and limits; final
  timbre and subjective mix balance still require phone-speaker and headphone listening.
- v2.10 intentionally contains no new sprite, texture, model, particle-system, soundtrack, or
  generated-art asset. Later graphics proposals must be reviewed as new work, not credited here.
