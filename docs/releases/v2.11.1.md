## Saved score contract

Save schema v3 keeps the existing wins, attempts, settings, global migration fallback, and
per-trial best times. It adds:

```text
lastScore: {
  grade, time, trial, attempt, damageDealt, woundsTaken
}
bestScores[trial]: {
  grade, time, trial, attempt, damageDealt, woundsTaken
}
```

`onBossDeath()` computes the grade and writes both the win and scorecard synchronously. The
per-trial `bestScores` entry follows the same best-time decision as `bests[trial]`; `lastScore`
always reflects the most recent victory. Existing v1 and v2 saves remain valid and gain these
fields on their next win.

## Result pacing

- The result card owns the screen for `4.5` simulation seconds.
- Confirmations made during the hold are consumed rather than queued.
- The replay prompt appears after the hold and requires a fresh click, tap, or Enter press.
- Prompt alpha stays between `0.64` and `0.88` on an approximately `5.5 s` cycle.
- The stable `SCORE SAVED` label remains visible independently of the replay pulse.
- The title readback includes the last saved grade.

## Changed from v2.11

- Save schema v2 becomes backward-compatible v3.
- Complete victory scorecards persist instead of only wins and best times.
- Victory cannot be skipped by a confirmation queued during the reveal.
- The score card holds longer and the replay prompt pulses more slowly and less deeply.
- No grade formula, combat rule, difficulty value, audio implementation, collision, character
  rendering, or runtime asset changes.
